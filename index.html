Overview

A Private Limited Company is one of the best Form of doing business in India as compared to LLP or OPC or Proprietorship or Partnership. Private Limited Company is a separate legal entity which is registered under the Companies Act, 2013. It is the most reliable and sought after by the entrepreneurs. Private Limited Company requires minimum two Shareholders. Shareholders are the owners of the Company. These 2 persons can also become Directors of the Company. The Shares of a Private Company is usually restricted to be transferred and prohibited to be traded at a Stock Exchange.

Small Businesses should register a Private Company because it is accepted everywhere and also impacts positively on the clients and other stakeholders. Also, since we all know that funds are needed to run a company and small Business often need funds from investors. Investors prefer a Private Limited Company than other form of business to fund. Foreign Investors also prefer a Private Limited Company to invest in a business. Tax rate is also reduced for a Private Limited Company as compared to LLP.

Features of a Private Limited Company

Members

To form a Private Limited Company minimum 2 shareholders are required and it can reach upto 20 shareholders

Directors

Only 2 Directors are required to form a company. Directors are the key employees of the company who work in the best interest of the company. Mostly the initial shareholders become the Directors of the Company

KNOW MORE

Permanent Existence

A Private Limited Company exists Forever until wound up with a due process. Even after the death of any of its shareholders or Directors a company can run its operation with help of a management.

KNOW MORE

Paid-up capital

There should be a minimum amount that should be available with company to be able to run. This Paid-Up Capital is received from the initial shareholders. There is no minimum or maximum cap on the paid-up capital of a company.

KNOW MORE

Liability

Liability of each shareholder is limited which means at the time of winding up, the Shareholders or the Owners of the Company do not require to sell their person assets to repay any debt.

KNOW MORE

Taxability

The Tax rate for a small Private Limited Company is 25% on its income For the Financial year 2021-22 with no surcharge. It is comparatively lesser than a LLP.
